## Demo Talking Points
- Shared physical cash is separate from provider balances.
- Provider-specific runway matters even when aggregate value looks healthy.
- Unusual activity is review-oriented, not a wrongdoing declaration.
- Missing or stale data reduces confidence visibly.
- Human ownership, acknowledgement, escalation, and closure are auditable.
- Reset/replay proves deterministic demo behavior.
