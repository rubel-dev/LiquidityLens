## ⏱️ Minute 0:00 – 0:40 | THE HOOK (The Eid Story)

**[Speaker faces judges — no screen yet, or show a black screen / title slide]**

> *In Mirpur-10, an agent named Karim Mia is running one of the busiest counters in the area.*
> *He serves bKash, Nagad, and Rocket customers — all from the same small shop.*
> *Right now, he has one cash drawer, three provider balances, and a line of customers outside.*
>*He just checked his dashboard. Everything looks fine. Total balance: ৳82,000. Green across the board.*
> *He has no idea that in 38 minutes, his bKash balance will hit zero.*
> *His field officer has no idea either.*
> *By the time they realize — customers are already turned away, complaints are filed, and Karim's rating drops.*
> *This is the problem we are solving."*

**[Pause 2 seconds. Let it land.]**

  Last 12 minutes — bKash cash-out activity:

  Txn #1   ৳4,950   Account: 01711-XXXX91   4:41 PM
  Txn #2   ৳4,980   Account: 01711-XXXX91   4:43 PM
  Txn #3   ৳4,920   Account: 01819-XXXX44   4:45 PM
  Txn #4   ৳5,010   Account: 01819-XXXX44   4:46 PM
  Txn #5   ৳4,970   Account: 01711-XXXX91   4:48 PM
  Txn #6   ৳4,990   Account: 01819-XXXX44   4:49 PM

  Account concentration ratio:  0.33  (normal baseline: 0.85+)
  Confidence: 71%   |   Uncertainty: HIGH

> *That is responsible AI."*

## ⏱️ Minute 4:30 – 5:00 | SCENARIO C — DATA DEGRADATION

**[Kill the Nagad mock API in background — or it was already degraded from setup]**

> *"Let me show you something most teams won't.*
> *What happens when a provider's data feed goes down?"*

**[Point to Nagad card — badge changes to yellow]**

```
  🟡 Nagad:   ৳ 45,000  ⚠️ Data delayed — 4 min 12 sec
               Estimate confidence: LOW
               Recommendation withheld pending fresh data
```

> *"Nagad's feed just went stale.*
> *Our system did not crash. It did not show a confident wrong number.*
> *It downgraded confidence to LOW and withheld the recommendation.*
> *The rest of the dashboard — bKash and Rocket — keeps working normally.*
> *When the feed recovers, confidence restores automatically."*

**[If time allows, restore the feed and show it going back to green]**

> *"This is called graceful degradation.*
> *Real production systems fail. Our prototype handles it honestly."*

## ⏱️ Minute 5:00 – 5:30 | FALSE POSITIVE — THE SYSTEM IS SMART ENOUGH NOT TO FLAG

**[Trigger the salary-day normal spike script]**

> *"One more thing before coordination.*
> *It is the 1st of the month. Salary day. Transactions spike — 14 cash-outs in 20 minutes.*
> *Watch what our system does."*

**[Show the system NOT firing an anomaly alert]**

```
  📊 Transaction spike detected — salary-day pattern
  Volume: 14 cash-outs in 20 minutes
  Status: Within expected salary-day baseline ✅
  Action: No alert raised — normal demand pattern
```

> *"No alert. Because our baseline knows the 1st of the month looks like this.*
> *A bad system would flag this as suspicious and waste the field officer's time.*
> *Our system knows the difference between an Eid spike, a salary day, and an anomaly.*
> *That is what reduces false positives — and that is what judges asked for."*

## ⏱️ Minute 5:30 – 7:00 | FULL COORDINATION LIFECYCLE

**[Switch to Ops Dashboard — Field Officer view]**

> *"Now let us switch to the field officer's view.*
> *Rafi, a bKash field officer, has just received two alerts for Karim's outlet."*

**[Show the alert queue with both alerts — liquidity + anomaly]**

> *"He clicks on the high-priority liquidity alert."*

**[Click into alert detail — show evidence, Bengali message, recommended action]**

**[Click "Acknowledge"]**

> *"He acknowledges it. Timestamp recorded: 4:52 PM.*
> *The system now shows this alert has an owner."*

**[Click "Assign to Risk Analyst"]**

> *"The anomaly alert is more complex — he escalates it to the risk analyst.*
> *He adds a case note."*

**[Type a short case note]**

```
Note: "Pattern flagged during Eid rush. Amounts clustered.
       Sent to risk team for review before next rebalance."
Timestamp: 4:53 PM  |  Owner: Risk Analyst — Tanvir Ahmed
```

**[Switch to Case Manager — Risk Analyst view]**

> *"Tanvir, the risk analyst, sees the case in his queue.*
> *He reviews the evidence panel — all 6 transactions, the account ratio, the confidence score.*
> *He decides: likely Eid demand, no further action needed.*
> *He adds a resolution note and closes the case."*

**[Click Resolve — show case status change]**

```
Case #1047  →  RESOLVED
Resolution: "Reviewed. Pattern consistent with Eid demand.
             No fraud indicators. Monitor for recurrence."
Resolved at: 4:58 PM  |  Total response time: 6 minutes
Audit trail: Open → Acknowledged → Escalated → Resolved  ✅
```

> *"Complete. Traceable. Every step logged.*
> *From alert firing to resolution in 6 minutes.*
> *No manual phone calls. No confusion about who owns it.*
> *This is not a notification — this is a coordination system."*

|---------|--------------|

| Wrong data shows | *"We are looking at our staging environment — let me switch to the seeded demo data."* |
